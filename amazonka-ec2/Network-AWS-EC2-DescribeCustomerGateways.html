Description

Describes one or more of your VPN customer gateways.

For more information about VPN customer gateways, see Adding a HardwareVirtual Private Gateway to Your VPC in the Amazon Virtual Private Cloud UserGuide.

http://docs.aws.amazon.com/AWSEC2/latest/APIReference/ApiReference-query-DescribeCustomerGateways.html
